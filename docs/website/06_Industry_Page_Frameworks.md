# Desklabs Industry Page Frameworks

![Industry framework](assets/industry_framework.png)

## Shared principle

Each industry page uses the same conversion architecture but changes the nouns, workflows, proof, screenshots, objections, and regulatory guardrails. Do not create five unrelated websites.

---

# 1. Travel & Tourism

## Positioning

**Run every traveler journey from inquiry to departure.**

## Audience

Tour operators, halal travel, open-trip operators, Umrah organizers, corporate travel, destination management, and travel agencies.

## Lifecycle

Inquiry → Qualification → Quotation → Booking → Payment → Documents → Manifest → Departure → After-sales.

## Relevant modules

Communication, CRM, package/quotation, booking, finance, document tracking, operations, Aurora AI.

## Hero proof scene

WhatsApp inquiry for Japan → Aurora extracts month, travelers, and budget → package recommendation → quotation prepared → booking status shown.

## Key pains

- inquiries distributed across sales phones
- package and pricing information inconsistent
- follow-up dependent on individual sales discipline
- booking, payment, passport, visa, and manifest data fragmented
- management cannot see conversion and workload clearly

## CTA

**Lihat Desklabs untuk Travel** / **Jadwalkan Demo Travel**

# 2. Education

## Positioning

**Turn every inquiry into a clear learner journey.**

## Audience

Schools, tutoring centers, course providers, bootcamps, language centers, training businesses, and education consultants.

## Lifecycle

Inquiry → Counseling → Application → Admission → Payment → Class/Program → Student support → Renewal.

## Relevant modules

Communication, CRM, admission pipeline, scheduling, payment, reminders, student/parent communication, automation, Aurora AI.

## Hero proof scene

Parent asks about program → inquiry assigned → qualification questions completed → trial class scheduled → payment reminder sent.

## Guardrail

Do not position Desklabs as a learning management system unless the product includes curriculum, assignment, and assessment capabilities. Position it as customer/student operations and communication.

# 3. Healthcare

## Positioning

**Make patient communication and service coordination easier to manage.**

## Audience

Clinics, dental practices, wellness providers, diagnostic services, and non-emergency care businesses.

## Lifecycle

Inquiry → Intake → Appointment → Reminder → Visit coordination → Follow-up → Return visit.

## Relevant modules

Communication, contact management, appointment workflow, reminders, consent/intake coordination, payment tracking, and operational AI assistance.

## Critical guardrail

Until formal legal and security readiness is established, do not claim:

- electronic medical record capability
- clinical decision support
- diagnosis or treatment recommendations
- storage of highly sensitive clinical data
- HIPAA compliance or equivalent certifications

Marketing should focus on non-clinical customer operations. Complete Indonesian PDP Law and industry compliance review before launch.

---

# 4. Property

## Positioning

**Move property leads from first message to signed deal without losing context.**

## Audience

Real-estate agencies, developers, brokers, property managers, and leasing teams.

## Lifecycle

Lead → Qualification → Unit matching → Viewing → Negotiation → Reservation → Payment → Handover → Relationship.

## Relevant modules

Communication, CRM, unit/deal workflow, appointment/viewing, document tracking, finance, automation, Aurora AI.

## Hero proof scene

Instagram lead → preferred location/budget extracted → viewing scheduled → unit recommendation → reservation tracked.

## Guardrail

Do not claim inventory availability or legal-document automation until those integrations are operational.

---

# 5. Agency & Professional Services

## Positioning

**Connect every client conversation to the work and revenue behind it.**

## Audience

Creative agencies, digital agencies, consultants, legal/accounting services, recruitment firms, and other project-based service businesses.

## Lifecycle

Lead → Discovery → Proposal → Negotiation → Project → Invoice → Delivery → Retainer/Renewal.

## Relevant modules

Communication, CRM, proposal/deal pipeline, project handoff, finance, notes, automation, Aurora AI.

## Hero proof scene

Inbound lead → requirements summarized → proposal task created → owner assigned → invoice and project handoff visible.
