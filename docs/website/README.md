# Desklabs Website Bible Pack v1.0

This package is the strategic, creative, content, motion, and implementation foundation for the new **desklabs.id**.

## Core decision

Desklabs is positioned as **the customer operations platform for modern service businesses**. Travel is the initial beachhead and proof market, but the homepage represents a horizontal platform that can serve:

- Travel & tourism
- Education
- Healthcare (non-clinical customer operations)
- Property
- Agencies and professional services

The reference supplied by Irfan is used as a benchmark for **narrative rhythm, product-led visuals, motion restraint, spacing, and conversion flow**. The design must not copy protected brand elements, copy, illustrations, or exact layouts.

## Recommended use

1. Approve positioning and homepage narrative in the master Bible.
2. Produce product visuals according to the asset brief.
3. Build the homepage shell and motion proof-of-concept.
4. Complete Indonesian copy first, then English localization.
5. Launch the platform homepage plus the Travel industry page.
6. Add Education, Property, Agency, then Healthcare after product/workflow readiness.

## Important strategic guardrail

Do not promise capabilities that do not exist. Healthcare messaging must stay within customer communication, scheduling, reminders, intake, and operational coordination until legal/compliance requirements for clinical data are formally met.
